Everyone is Italian on May 16 & 17 when the nation's oldest outdoor market celebrates its annual Italian Market Festival.


Bring the entire family and enjoy all the Festival Events, including Live Musical Entertainment, Arts and Crafts, Half Ball Tournament, Grease Pole Contest, The Traditional Procession of Saints, and all the delicious food.

Events

  • Traditional Pasta Making Classes

    With our pasta making classes in Philadelphia, you can learn a new skill that is truly impressive. There is nothing quite like homemade pasta. Our highly skilled chefs will go through the pasta making process with you, such as kneading the dough, rolling out the noodles, and then cooking to perfection. Discover new types of pasta that will change your life. With pasta, you usually want sauce. Learn how to whip up a variety of accompanying sauces to make your final homemade pasta dish absolutely superb.

  • The Procession of Saints

    Father John Large leads this year's procession, which has long been a customary part of the annual parish May celebration. Saint Mary Magdalen de' Pazzi Church, School, and Orphanage c. 1906. The Procession of Saints starts at the historic Saint Mary Magdalen de' Pazzi Church (712 Montrose St.), and pauses for the Blessing of the Market at 9th and Washington. The procession concludes at St. Paul Catholic Church (808 S. Hutchinson, on Christian St. between 9th & 10th). This event will occur rain or shine!

  • Auto Image Car Show

    Classic cars of every description will line up along Park Street between Cross St and Wilder St. See antique roadsters, coupes, hot rods, muscle cars, and much more. Most of the cars come from the magical decades of the '50s and the '60s, but there will also be plenty of muscle cars from the '70s and '80s along with classic beauties from the '20s, '30s, and '40s. Bring your camera and don't be afraid to talk to the owners -- they love to talk about their cars.

  • Dane Anthony Bands

    This versatile core group of five accomplished musicians are Atlantic City lounge legends! They can make you sing, dance and clap your hands to the ‘boogity’ beat whether it’s pop, rock, blues, or R&B (and sometimes a little bit country). Whatever your favorite genre, this band can cover it in their own unique style, put a smile on your face, and surely start a party!
