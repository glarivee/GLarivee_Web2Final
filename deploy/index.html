The Italian Market Festival

Everyone is Italian on May 16 & 17 when the nation's oldest outdoor market celebrates its annual Italian Market Festival. Bring the entire family and enjoy all the Festival Events, including Live Musical Entertainment, Arts and Crafts, Half Ball Tournament, Grease Pole Contest, The Traditional Procession of Saints, and all the delicious food.
